October 2016

Three Important Actions
Dear Leaders,

Exciting things are happening with the World Forum Foundation. Our Program Development Team, Dimensions Events Team, Administrative Partner NZTC, Sponsors, WF Alliance members and WF Leadership are all working hard planning 2017 World Forum, building registrations and constructing a powerful program. WF Working Groups (see Focused Work on website) are engaged project development and Global Leaders cohorts are being assembled. We need you, as WF Leaders, to share information and updates with your networks. Here are the top 3 actions for October!

2. Registration Incentive
Registering for 2017 World Forum before December 31, 2016 will make a difference for everyone. A generous donor has agreed to match a portion of these registration fees to build the World Forum Fellowship Fund. This Fund allows individuals with limited resources to attend World Forum Events. Reach out to your networks, friends and colleagues and encourage them to take advantage of this opportunity to build the global strength and diversity of the World Forum.

Click here to register.

3. Connection Center Conversations
The Connection Center on the World Forum website is now open to users, without needing to enter login information. Two areas in the Connection Center, 1) Global Perspectives (which includes Bonnie's Global Cafe and Global Leaders), and 2) Focused Work are available to everyone. However, in order to continue to promote safe and secure discussions for the World Forum Community, our users will still need to login to access the Conversations.
